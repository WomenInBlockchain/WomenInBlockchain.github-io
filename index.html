OUR MISSION Disrupt The Industry
Through Diversity

There are few female leaders in the blockchain ecosystem. With the potential use cases of blockchain-based products being particularly vast, so are opportunities for women.

Although a technical skill set is the most in demand from many businesses, it is not a requirement of a blockchain expert. In fact, women in blockchain have taken up various roles - from technical, academic, commercial, regulatory, and public relations.

Obtaining technical knowledge on the technology can serve as challenge, especially when the information is varied and scattered across the internet. Women in Blockchain Global hopes help women over this steep learning curve.

So Why Are We #Hacking Blockchain Tech

As businesses begin to realize the potential of blockchain-based systems, the demand for talent within in the industry has grown tremendously. Banks and insurers are beginning to test blockchain projects. Venture Capitalist have invested over $1.4 billion in blockchain startups. And with the quantity of interest comes the unfortunate halt in innovation - the lack of developers and enthusiasts who have mastery knowledge of the technology.

The future of blockchain technology looks bright, however, there's an evident shortage of knowledgeable individuals within the space. We strive to not only close gender gaps but to fill the void by building an 'army' of blockchain masters.

  • More Than The Technology

  • Build
